How to use LaTex in html?#

You can follow the tutorials:

  1. Hugo 静态博客中迄今为止最佳数学公式支持-利用 MathJax & MMark
  2. MathJax Support

The reason why MMark is recommended is that you can start writing mathematical formulas without more configuration. The mathematical formulas are all surrounded by two dollar signs $, like this $$ f(x) = sin(x) $$​​, as shown The effect is \(f(x) = sin(x) \)​​. In-line formulas and inter-line formulas are the same. The difference is that a blank line is added before and after the inter-line formulas so that Mmark can automatically determine whether you need an in-line formula or an inter-line formula. Examples of formulas between lines:

$$
f(x) = sin(x)
$$

The display effect is \( f(x) = sin(x) \)
